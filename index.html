Future of Coding

Hello! I'm Steve Krouse. Welcome to my podcast and open-journal research project to create the future of coding.

Every week (or so) I release a new podcast, alternating between recapping my own research over the past two weeks and interviewing programming experts.

If you have something to add to the conversation, don't hesitate to reach out on Github, Twitter or email me via steve at futureofcoding.org.
